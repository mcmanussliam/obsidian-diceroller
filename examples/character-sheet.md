# Brumdar Ironsnout, Dwarven Hog Rider

*Medium humanoid (dwarf), chaotic neutral*

**Armour Class** 16 (splint mail)
**Hit Points** 65 (10d8 + 20)
**Speed** 40 ft. (mounted on Gristle)

---

## Ability Scores

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 18 (+4) | 12 (+1) | 16 (+3) | 8 (−1) | 12 (+1) | 10 (+0) |

**Proficiency Bonus** +3
**Saving Throws** STR +7, CON +6
**Skills** Animal Handling +4, Athletics +7, Intimidation +3
**Senses** Darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 11
**Languages** Common, Dwarvish

---

## Traits

**Dwarven Resilience.** Advantage on saving throws against poison.

**Mounted Fury.** While mounted on Gristle, Brumdar's melee attacks deal an extra 1d6 damage.

**Trampling Charge.** If Brumdar moves at least 20 ft. straight toward a creature while mounted and hits with a tusk attack, the target must succeed on a DC 14 STR saving throw or be knocked prone.

---

## Actions

**Multiattack.** Brumdar makes two attacks: one with his war pick and one with his handaxe.

**War Pick.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* `1d20+STR+prof` to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: `1d8+STR` piercing damage, plus `1d6` while mounted.

**Handaxe.** *Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack:* `1d20+STR+prof` to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: `1d6+STR` slashing damage.

**Gristle's Gore (Recharge 5–6).** Brumdar directs his war hog to gore a target. *Melee Weapon Attack:* `1d20+STR+prof` to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: `2d6+STR` piercing damage.

---

## Reactions

**Bristle Shield.** When a creature misses Brumdar with a melee attack, he can roll `1d4` and add the result to his AC until the start of his next turn.

**Tusk.** `1d20+5` to hit. Hit: `2d6+3` slashing.
